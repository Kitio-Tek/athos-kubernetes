# Developer Certificate of Origin

By signing off on a commit, you certify that the contribution is your work or
is made under the terms of the [Developer Certificate of Origin](https://developercertificate.org/).

Sign off in the commit message:

```bash
git commit -s -m "Add a feature"
```

The trailing line `Signed-off-by: Your Name <you@example.com>` is the
certification token.
